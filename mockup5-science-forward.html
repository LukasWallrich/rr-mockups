Open Access Journal

Advancing Science Through Replication

Replication Research (R²) is an open-access, peer-reviewed journal dedicated to publishing replication and reproduction studies across all scientific disciplines. We provide rigorous, transparent evaluation of empirical findings to strengthen the foundations of cumulative knowledge. All submissions undergo reproducibility checks and open peer review to ensure the highest methodological standards.

Core Principles

// How we ensure quality and transparency

Active

Open & Persistent Peer Review

Every review is published alongside the article with reviewer identities disclosed. Reviews remain permanently accessible, fostering accountability and scholarly dialogue.

Verified

Reproducibility Checks

Submitted analyses are independently verified for computational reproducibility. Data and code must be openly shared, and results are confirmed before publication.

Diamond OA

No Article Processing Charges

R² operates as a Diamond Open Access journal. Authors pay nothing to publish, and readers pay nothing to access. Science should be free for everyone.
