About the Journal

Replication Research (R2) is a diamond open-access journal dedicated to publishing replication and reproduction studies across all scholarly disciplines. We believe that verifying and building upon existing findings is essential to the integrity and progress of science. All articles undergo rigorous peer review and are freely available to readers worldwide, with no fees charged to authors.

Key Features

  • 1

    Open & Persistent Peer Review

    Reviews are published alongside articles, ensuring transparency and accountability throughout the editorial process.

  • 2

    Reproducibility Checks

    All submissions with quantitative analyses undergo independent computational reproducibility verification.

  • 3

    No Article Processing Charges

    Diamond open access means free to read, free to publish — removing financial barriers for researchers everywhere.
