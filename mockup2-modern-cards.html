Open Peer Review

Transparent, community-driven peer review. All reviews are published alongside articles, ensuring accountability and fostering constructive scholarly dialogue.

Reproducibility First

Every submission must include data and analysis code. Computational reproducibility checks are part of the review process, raising the bar for scientific rigour.

No APCs Ever

Diamond Open Access means no article processing charges for authors and no paywalls for readers. Science should be freely accessible to everyone, always.
