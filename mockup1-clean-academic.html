Diamond Open Access for Replication Science

Replication Research (R²) is a peer-reviewed, diamond open access journal dedicated to publishing replication and reproducibility studies across the sciences and humanities. We charge no fees to authors or readers, believing that rigorous replication is a public good that strengthens the foundations of cumulative knowledge.

Key Features

Open & Persistent Peer Review

All reviews are published alongside articles, ensuring transparency and accountability throughout the editorial process.

Reproducibility Checks

Submitted analyses undergo independent computational reproducibility verification before publication.

No Article Processing Charges

Diamond open access means no costs for authors or readers — ever. Funded by institutional support, not paywalls.
