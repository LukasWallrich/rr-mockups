Advancing Science Through Replication

Free to read, free to publish

Replication Research (R²) is an open-access, peer-reviewed journal dedicated to publishing replication studies, reproducibility assessments, and meta-scientific investigations across all empirical disciplines.

Learn More Submit Your Work

Why Publish With Us

01

Open & Persistent Peer Review

All reviews are published alongside articles with persistent identifiers, ensuring full transparency and giving credit to reviewers for their scholarly contributions.

02

Reproducibility Checks

Every submission undergoes rigorous computational reproducibility verification. We ensure that analyses can be independently reproduced from shared data and code.

03

No Article Processing Charges

Publishing should not depend on funding. R² is diamond open access — completely free for both authors and readers, removing financial barriers to scientific communication.
